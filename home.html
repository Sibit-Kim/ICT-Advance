KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP SINCE 1992

Kakuma Refugee camp was established in the year 1992 with the displace community from south sudan due to heavy war in the southern part of the country, Loki was the main reception area of the new arrival before the establishment of Kakuma Refugee camp in the year 1992. Today Kakuma is made up different nationalities that flee from their country due to several reasons, here are the following nationalities from kakuma, south sudanese, sudanese, nigerians, ugandan, congolese, ethiopians, rwandans, burundians, and somalians
